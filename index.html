About AU Ignite

Partnering with excellence to build a skilled workforce for tomorrow

Our Mission

AU Foundation, the CSR arm of AU Small Finance Bank, has partnered with Ambuja Foundation to launch the Future Skills Academy. We're dedicated to empowering marginalized youth with relevant IT skills to transform their careers and communities.

5 Years of Impact

Celebrating five years of collaboration, we've established seven skill training centers across Rajasthan, benefitting 5,500+ rural youth. Our expanded reach now includes Jaipur, Chirawa, Jaitaran, Nagaur, and Goverdhan.

Our Commitment

Through comprehensive training in 43 organized sectors, we're working towards our goal of training 28,000 youth by 2025. Every program includes essential computer skills, functional English, and professional soft skills development.
